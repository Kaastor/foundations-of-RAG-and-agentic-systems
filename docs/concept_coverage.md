## Self-audit

- Every chapter from 1 to 39 is represented either in executable code or in explicit documentation.
- No major textbook theme is silently ignored. Where the repository does not implement the real production mechanism, the gap is named directly in the **Notes** column.
- The largest simplifications are: hashed vectors instead of neural embeddings; LSH instead of HNSW/IVF/PQ; extractive synthesis instead of an LLM; toy governance and security policies instead of enterprise control planes; and file-based traces/caches instead of distributed infrastructure.
- Chapters 39 and the most advanced parts of 7, 8, 18, 28, 32, 34, 36, and 38 are the main areas where the code is intentionally pedagogical rather than fully faithful to production reality.
