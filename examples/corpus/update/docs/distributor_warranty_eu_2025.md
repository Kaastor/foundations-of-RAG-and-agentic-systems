# Exclusions
EU distributor terms for V14 exclude damage from unapproved seal replacements, unsupported coastal cleaning chemicals, and telemetry vendors that do not keep twelve months of access logs.

# Change note
The 2025 terms add the telemetry-vendor logging requirement.
