# Approval threshold
The contractor travel approval threshold for Poland is 900 EUR effective in Q1 2025.

# Approval chain
Amounts above the threshold require manager approval and finance notification.

# Change note
This policy replaces the 600 EUR threshold from the 2024 Poland policy.
